    About ACM Richmond

    A professional chapter of the Association for Computing Machinery, rooted in Richmond, VA.

    Our Mission

    ACM Richmond exists to advance the computing profession in the greater Richmond area. We host expert-led talks, hands-on workshops, and collaborative events that help local technologists grow their skills, stay current with the industry, and build meaningful professional connections.

    We are affiliated with the global Association for Computing Machinery, the world's largest computing professional society. Our chapter brings ACM's global knowledge network and standards to the Richmond tech community.

    Our Values

    🔬 Technical Excellence

    We prioritize substance over hype — curating content that is rigorous, practical, and grounded in real engineering experience.

    🤝 Inclusive Community

    Every event is free and open to all. We welcome students, career-changers, junior engineers, and seasoned veterans equally.

    🔒 Integrity & Trust

    Our speakers are vetted by officers. Content is controlled via the ACM Richmond GitHub repository — no anonymous submissions.
