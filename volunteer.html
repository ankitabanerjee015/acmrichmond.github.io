    Help make ACM Richmond events a success. Browse open slots and sign up below.

    Volunteer Roles

    🎤

    Event Moderator

    Introduce speakers, manage Q&A, keep sessions on time. Great for people who enjoy public engagement.

    📷

    Social Media

    Capture event highlights, post live updates, and help document the chapter's activities on LinkedIn and YouTube.

    📦

    Logistics

    Set up the venue, manage sign-ins, coordinate with speakers, and ensure everything runs smoothly.

    💻

    Workshop Facilitator

    Guide participants through hands-on exercises, answer questions, and help maintain a productive workshop flow.

    How to sign up: Click a highlighted date (amber) on the calendar to see available slots for that date. Each slot has a sign-up button that links to a Google Form. Spots are limited — sign up early!
